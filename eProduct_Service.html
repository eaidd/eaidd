Products and Services


The company is the Mednolia company, located in Boston, USA, and can provide at least four services 
for pharmaceutical companies, research institutes and universities:

1. Calculate the full electronic structure of the protein, that is, the full wave function 
(molecular orbitals) and the corresponding energy level. The commissioning unit can be calculated 
from some properties of the protein;

2. According to the needs of customers, on the basis of Service 1, increase the calculation of 
protein active pockets, active residues and active atoms;

3. On the basis of service 1,2, add a batch of drug molecules provided by customers, we will calculate 
the binding affinity of drug molecules;

4. It can calculate the change of conductivity after protein-protein interaction for customers.

The above services are subject to signing a contract.
