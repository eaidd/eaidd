Quantum Mechanical Calculations on Protein-Drug Binding Sites

and it is the first and important step in drug design. If there is a deviation in the understanding of the binding
site, the entire drug discovery and research work will go astray. But for a long time,only use experiments for 
drug screening. 
         
Just as the hydrogen spectrum must be measured experimentally in the early days, the hydrogen spectrum can be 
calculated later by quantum mechanics. We have developed the quantum mechanics calculation of the full electronic 
structure of the protein and established the three necessary and sufficient conditions for the binding site.  
Thus, the quantum mechanical calculation for the binding site of protein-drug is completed, and the discovery of the 
binding site is based on a solid basic science.
